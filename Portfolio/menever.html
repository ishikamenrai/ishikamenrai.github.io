#MeNever Inner Conflict

This film is a personal project. The idea behind it is a campaign against drug abuse called #MeNever. The film aims to show that a single slip in your weakest moments can cost you your every dream. Drugs are just chemicals that can catch you. Don't get sucked in. It shows the inner conflict that goes on in the minds of victims of substance abuse/ dependence.
