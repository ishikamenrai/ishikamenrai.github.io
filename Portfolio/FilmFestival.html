#30 Minute Film Festival Website

This project was aimed at creating a website to host the film created by a group of students in a 30 minute film festival. The film was made by a group of 3 students in a total of 40 minutes- 10 minutes for the creating the concept and 30 minutes to shoot and edit the film. This website was created as to host this film. It was an individual assignment and the first of this semester.
