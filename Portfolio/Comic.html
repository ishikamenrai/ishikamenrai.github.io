A Love Letter- Webcomic

This assignment was part of the class for Communications Lab. It was a group assignment aimed at creating an interactive comic hosted on a webiste. The theme of our assignment is that a boy writes a heart-felt letter to his best friend to confess his feelings to her, only to figure out someone else got there first. He folds the letter into a paper airplane and throws it from the roof. The website moves through their life story while slowly unfolding the content of the letter.
